Pizza

How to make a homemade pizza

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 Â½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    Steps
  1. Step One

    2. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan.

  2. Step Two

    3. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.

  3. Step Three

    4. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.

  4. Step Four

    5. Let rest for 5 minutes.

  5. Step Five

    6. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.

  6. Step Six

    7. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.

  7. Step Seven

    8. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.

  8. Step Eight

    9. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
